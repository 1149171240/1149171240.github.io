Cryptocurrencies

发表于 2019-08-22 | 分类于 English | 阅读数
字数统计 1.8k | 阅读时长 7

单词

  • cryptocurrencies是cryptocurrency的负数形式，currency是通用的，流通的意思，前面的crypty有密码，加密的意思
  • frenzy=crazy
  • pretender动词形式是pretend假装，六级必考词汇。tend呢
  • throne王座，权利的游戏 game of throne;
  • underpinning, pinning大头针的意思，在大头针下面的东西肯定是牢固的，基础的东西。
  • transaction交易，每一次淘宝都是一个transaction
  • token硬币就是token，还有个taken，这个单词梦的解析那篇文章前的一篇，写猴子也有嫉妒心的那篇文章出现过
  • flourishing繁荣的，flush与blush跟这个单词有点类似，一起记住都是脸红的意思
  • tweak强调的微调，weak虚弱的，虚弱的人只能慢慢挪动，就是微调
  • principle原理 theory
  • concrete具体的，水泥，抽象的呢abstract
  • quirks
  • constitute六级这单词必须会，constitution宪法,institute表示机构，都是六级高频词汇了
  • giant
  • startup这个单词六级考的概率很大
  • du jour这单词有意思，感觉像法语
  • juvenile这个单词考研应该经常碰到哈，近似young naive
  • decentralised centralized
  • fancy和上面giant意思一样,但字面上有想象的，美好的意思
  • unilaterally单方面的，
  • downside=side effect= weakness
  • pirate盗版，海盗 private跟这个单词一起看，容易混
  • unethical ethical这个单词我通常与aesthetic一起记
  • technically
  • Periodically 这次有大姨妈的意思，on someone’s periods
  • semi-randomly半随机

    短语

  • caused a financial frenzy形容引发潮流，比popular好
  • In a word一般用在作文最后一句吧
  • upon rules of the network,这里的upon等与based on了
  • fulfill the same purpose,fulfill一般跟计划，梦想搭配，表示完成了
  • take the same principle，相同原理不用have用take，可以记一下，还有个类似短语on the principle
  • collective agreement共同协议，collective与原型动词collect区别很大，表示集体的意思,不过也好理解，收集的东西组成一个小群体
  • sit in my digital wallet在我的钱包，居然可以用sit这个单词
  • For instance
  • have much to do with这个词组经常出现在阅读的提问中
  • rather more limited.特别是rather more的使用==especially
  • distinction with 本意是区别，文章引申为优点
  • freeze your account冻结账户
  • cripple your business,cripple本意是瘸子，残废==lame
  • host pirated videos
  • lies at the heart of
  • up-to-date
  • out of kilter with sth与某某格格不入
  • Hard landing
  • Cruising altitude 学了高度，肯定还要学纬度latitude,cruise这个单词要回，海边去玩都有落日巡游项目，就是用的这个

    句子

  • Bitcoin was the first cryptocurrency, and is still the biggest, but in the eight years since it was created pretenders to the throne have come along.这句话挺复杂，首先有三个态了，过去怎么样，现在是怎么样的。然后最后一句，我觉得可以加个逗号，in the eight years since it was created, pretenders to the throne have come.因为有since所以用完成时。
  • What you really have when you own a bitcoin is the collective agreement of every other computer on the bitcoin network that your bitcoin was legitimately created by a bitcoin “miner”, and then passed on to you through a series of legitimate transactions.
    这句话很长，简单看就是what you have when you own a bitcoin is,然后再后面一大串就是修饰你have的从句，你have什么呢，最终have的就是一个agreement，这个agreement有啥用，又是一个从句从that your bitcoin was legitimately到最后。
  • For instance, since the first bitcoin was created in 2009, the total number in existence has been growing slowly, at a declining rate, ensuring that at some point around 2140, the 21 millionth bitcoin will be mined, and no more will ever be created.
    这句写法可以get，一般人顶多写个growing slowly就结束了，结果作者又形容了下growing的速度，at a declining rate.ensuring后面再接个从句，表示比特币创造最终的时间。这句话必须掌握，肯定很好再考场用。
  • In theory, almost anything that can be done with a computer could, in some way, be rebuilt on a cryptocurrency-based platform.
  • In effect, a giant hive-mind PC (that this no longer sounds like it has much to do with “currencies” is part of the reason some instead suggest the name “decentralised apps” to cover this sector).这句话应该缺少标点了，回头细谈。
  • The only authority on the network is whatever the majority of bitcoin users agree on, and in practice that means nothing more than the basic rules of the network are ever enforced.
    一般自己写作文，为了句式多样性，肯定要写一个头轻脚重的句子，比如这种以only开头，唯一的权威是users的共识，一般人写到这里就没了，但作者又进一步描述了，这种权威意味这什么,that means是指权威意味这什么，而不是指共识意味着什么，特别注意这点。
  • The flip side of cryptocurrencies being decentralised databases is that for most people, most of the time, there’s no downside to a centralised database.
    flip本意是翻转的，flip bird但这里flip side翻译成特点，我查阅了资料，flip side更确切的是接近on the other hand意思，这句话前一句说It is a lot about crime.后面flip side表明，另一方面，它又是那么“无罪的”。我觉得这里公众号翻译有点欠妥。
  • That’s why bitcoin, for instance, has become the currency of choice for online drug dealers and cybercriminals demanding ransoms to restore hacked data.
    //以后你写that’s why sth…的句子，考虑下，是不是可以加个for instance，提升下 drug虽然又药的意思，但是除了作为毒品，我很少见正统文章关于这个单词的其他用法。
  • “Crime” is a broad term, though.
    though放在逗号后面，逼格提升了好多。
  • There is if you take the more hostile, second answer to be correct:that collective greed has fuelled a speculative bubble that will eventually come crashing down.
    谁说there be句式低级呢，我就没看懂这个there be咋用的。其实这句话不用there is,就直接说if…也毫无问题。我网上还么找到这种用法的例子。后面bubble又接了个从句，说么这个bubble最终会怎么样。这种写法可以模仿了。该模仿的句式，会练习写作前一起讲。
  • Or this speculative bubble could end with a crash so severe that it destroys faith in the entire sector, driving the investors out, bankrupting the miners who’ve spent thousands or millions on single-purpose hardware that requires a high bitcoin price to turn a profit, and leaving cryptocurrencies as a technological dead-end alongside cold fusion and jetpacks.
  • Cryptocurrencies’ actual use stays stable, mostly illegal, largely underground, and completely disconnected from a market price that fluctuates wildly based on the whims of a class of financial speculators with little link to the ground truth.这句话厉害的地方在于，都是副词+形容词/动词，层层递进。从illegal到underground再到disconnect, ground truth表示真相，以后论文里面碰到就是表示正确的数据。that fluctuates这个修饰的是虚拟货币的use，这个use因为speculato(我觉得这个就是指投机人，而不是什么金融预测员)对于真相的不足联系而造成的。
